Percentage of People With Disabilities Who Commute by Car Alone
According to the U.S. Census Bureau, commuting by private vehicle includes driving in a car, truck or van to work. Darker colored states indicate a greater percent of people using a private vehicle to go to work. The lowest state was District of Columbia (DC) at 32.3%. The highest state was Alabama (AL) at 81.4%.
